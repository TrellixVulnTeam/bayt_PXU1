SON Neighbor Tables

SON neighbor tables are created by interacting with multiple types of neighbor devices.

  • UE-reported neighbors - A UE attached to a cell can report neighboring cells it detects.
  • X2-reported neighbors - Other cells can contact a cell to report themselves as neighbors. This happens often as a result of UE measurements from UEs attached to the external cells. The SON X2 Gateway service obtains Neighbor Relation Table (PW EMS) updates for cell information from the X2GW (HNG) over the REST interface.
  • Configuration-defined neighbors - Statically defined neighbors are established at the time of configuration and assumed to be valid.
